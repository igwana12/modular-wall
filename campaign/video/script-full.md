# Full Campaign Video Script

**Runtime:** 2-3 minutes
**Purpose:** Primary Kickstarter campaign video
**Structure:** Hook → Problem → Solution Demo → How It Works → Maker Story → Tiers and CTA
**Tone:** Mysterious opening, then conversational and transparent

---

## 1. HOOK (0-10s)

**VISUAL:** (CLOSE-UP) Spirit Sphere glowing in darkness. Ethereal pulsing light. Volumetric deity face materializes inside the sphere.

**AUDIO:** Deep ambient hum. Then Zeus's voice emerges.

**ZEUS VOICE:** "You summoned me. What weighs on your mind?"

**VISUAL:** (WIDE) Pull back to reveal full Spirit Sphere on a desk.

**VOICEOVER:** "What if mythology could speak to you personally?"

## Production Notes

### Camera Setup
- **Talking head:** Eye-level, softbox lighting from 45-degree angles, clean background
- **Product shots:** Overhead and side angles, low ambient light for glow visibility
- **B-roll:** Mix of screen recordings, hardware close-ups, assembly footage

### Lighting
- **Spirit Sphere demos:** Low ambient light (maximize LED visibility)
- **Talking head:** Even, soft lighting (no harsh shadows)
- **Hardware close-ups:** Directional lighting to reveal PCB detail

### Audio Layers
- **Voiceover:** Clear, authoritative, warm tone — primary audio track
- **Deity voices:** +2dB above voiceover when speaking, reverb for depth
- **Ambient soundscape:** Subtle, 30% volume, throughout
- **Music:** Optional — ethereal/ambient, low volume, never overpowers voice

### Text Overlays
- **Product specs:** Clean sans-serif, high contrast, readable at mobile size
- **Pricing:** Bold, large, centered
- **URL/CTA:** Bottom-center, white on dark

### Timing Flexibility
- **Minimum viable:** 2:00 (cut B-roll, tighten talking head)
- **Optimal:** 2:30 (full script as written)
- **Maximum:** 3:00 (add more deity demos, expand maker story)

### Shooting Priority

**READY NOW (no hardware needed):**
- Talking head segments (maker story)
- Text overlay plates
- Voiceover recording
- Screen recordings (web app, GitHub repo)
- B-roll (books, museum shots can be stock footage)

**NEEDS HARDWARE (blocked until prototypes ready):**
- Spirit Sphere glow and POV display in action
- Oracle Engine voice conversation demo
- PCB close-up shots with components visible
- Assembly process footage

**POST-PRODUCTION (no hardware required):**
- Animated flow chart (how it works section)
- Color grading
- Audio mixing and mastering
- Text overlay timing
- Final export and compression

## Key Messages

1. **Hook:** Mythology can speak to you personally
2. **Problem:** Ancient wisdom is locked away, hard to access
3. **Solution:** Voice AI oracle with 21 Greek deities
4. **Differentiation:** Open-source Maker Edition, not consumer appliance
5. **Transparency:** First-time hardware maker, learning in public
6. **CTA:** Early bird pricing limited to first 500 backers

---

## Value Reversals (McKee Principles)

- **Opening:** Mythology is distant and inaccessible
- **Mid-point:** Technology makes mythology conversational
- **Climax:** You can hack, modify, and personalize the experience (open-source empowerment)
- **Resolution:** The gods are waiting — mythology is now a living relationship
