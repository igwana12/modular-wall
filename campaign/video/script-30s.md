# 30-Second Demo Video Script

**Runtime:** 30 seconds
**Purpose:** Teaser clip for social media, email campaigns, and campaign page hero section
**Tone:** Mysterious → wonder → clarity

---

## Script Breakdown

### 0-3s: HOOK
**VISUAL:** (CLOSE-UP) Spirit Sphere glowing in darkness. Ethereal pulsing light. Camera slowly pulls back.

**AUDIO:** Ambient soundscape — deep, ethereal hum. Then a voice emerges, ancient and commanding.

**ZEUS VOICE:** "The path you seek was never hidden — you were simply afraid to look."

---

### 3-10s: REVEAL
**VISUAL:** (WIDE) Full Spirit Sphere on a desk. POV globe spinning with volumetric deity animation visible inside. Soft blue-gold glow.

**VOICEOVER:** "This is The Orb."

**AUDIO:** Ambient swells. Light musical accent.

---

### 10-20s: DEMO
**VISUAL:** Quick cuts (2-3 seconds each):
1. (MEDIUM) Person speaking to Oracle Engine on desk
2. (CLOSE-UP) Oracle Engine glowing, deity voice responding
3. (WIDE) Spirit Sphere POV display showing animated deity face during reading

**VOICEOVER:** "21 Greek deities. Real AI conversations. Open-source hardware."

**AUDIO:** Voice continues under deity response audio (Zeus speaking briefly).

---

### 20-27s: PRODUCTS
**VISUAL:** (WIDE) Side-by-side product shot:
- Left: Oracle Engine (voice AI device)
- Right: Spirit Sphere (full POV system)

**TEXT OVERLAY:**
```
Oracle Engine — $79 Early Bird
Spirit Sphere — $179 Early Bird
```

**VOICEOVER:** "Oracle Engine at $79. Spirit Sphere at $179. Early bird pricing for first 500 backers."

---

### 27-30s: CTA
**VISUAL:** (CLOSE-UP) Spirit Sphere glow pulses once, then fades to black.

**TEXT OVERLAY:**
```
Back us on Kickstarter
oracleball.ai
```

**VOICEOVER:** "The gods are waiting."

**AUDIO:** Ambient fades to silence.

---

## Production Notes

### Camera Angles
- All shots horizontal orientation (16:9)
- Hook and CTA: tight close-up on Spirit Sphere glow
- Demo: mix of medium (person at desk) and close-up (device detail)
- Products: wide two-shot with clean background

### Lighting
- Low ambient light for Spirit Sphere glow shots (maximize LED visibility)
- Softbox lighting for person speaking (eliminate harsh shadows)
- Neutral desk background for product shots

### Audio Mixing
- Zeus voice: +3dB, reverb/echo for depth
- Voiceover: clear, centered, authoritative tone
- Ambient soundscape: subtle, not overpowering
- Deity response audio in demo: intelligible but brief (1-2 seconds max)

### Text Overlay Specs
- Font: Sans-serif, bold, high contrast
- Pricing: large, readable at mobile size
- URL: bottom-center, white text on dark background

### Shooting Priority
- **READY NOW:** Text overlay plates, voiceover recording
- **NEEDS HARDWARE:** All Spirit Sphere glow shots, Oracle Engine voice demo, POV display animation
- **POST-PRODUCTION:** Timing, color grading, audio mixing

---

## Key Messages
1. **Mystery**: What is this glowing object?
2. **Wonder**: It speaks with the voices of Greek gods
3. **Clarity**: Two products, clear pricing, accessible via Kickstarter
