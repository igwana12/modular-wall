## Technical Specs

### Camera Settings (All Shots)
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160) — allows cropping and reframing in post
- **Frame rate:** 24fps (cinematic) or 30fps (web standard)
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (horizontal orientation)
- **Exposure:** Manual mode for consistent lighting across shots

### Lighting Setups
- **NEEDS_HARDWARE shots (glow visibility):** Low ambient light, <10 lux, LED glow as primary light source
- **Talking head shots:** Two softbox lights at 45-degree angles, fill light to reduce shadows
- **B-roll (books, desk):** Natural or diffused lighting, avoid harsh shadows

### Audio Recording
- **Voiceover:** Lavalier mic or USB condenser mic, quiet room, 48kHz 24-bit WAV
- **Deity voices:** Pre-recorded via ElevenLabs, exported as 48kHz 24-bit WAV
- **Ambient soundscape:** Royalty-free or original composition, 48kHz 24-bit WAV

### Export Formats
- **Master:** 4K ProRes or H.265 (10-bit color)
- **Kickstarter upload:** 1080p H.264, <500MB file size
- **Social media:** 1080p (Instagram), 720p (Twitter), vertical 9:16 crops for TikTok/Reels
