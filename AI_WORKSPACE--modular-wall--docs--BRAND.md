## Product Name Decision (Issue #1)

### The Name: mosAIc

**Rationale**:
- **Mosaic** = ancient art form where small tiles create a larger picture
- **AI** embedded directly in the name (capital A, capital I)
- Each module is a tile; together they form an intelligent mosaic
- Ties to Sacred Circuits' mythology + technology fusion aesthetic
- Short, memorable, unique in the smart home / modular display space

### Casing Rules

**Always use**:
- Logo/brand: `mosAIc` (lowercase m, capital A and I)
- In running text: "mosAIc" or "the mosAIc wall"
- Website URL: `mosaic.ai` or `getmosaic.io` (check availability)

**Never use**:
- ❌ "Mosaic" (regular capitalization)
- ❌ "MOSAIC" (all caps)
- ❌ "MosAIc" (capital M)
- ❌ "mosaIc" (lowercase AI)
- ❌ "The Modular Wall" (old placeholder)
- ❌ "[MODULAR]" (placeholder)

## Visual Style Decision (Issue #2)

### Selected Style: Minimal (Cyberpunk)

**Rationale**:
- Aligns with Council recommendation for "cyberpunk/transparent aesthetic"
- Clean lines, industrial aesthetic
- Smoke-tinted acrylic (invisible when off, glows when on)
- Visible electronics as decoration (not hidden)
- "Stealth wealth" for technology — understated until activated
- Matches Sacred Circuits brand (tech + mythology, not consumer electronics)

### Design Principles

**Materials**:
- Primary: Gray smoke acrylic #2064 (Canal Plastics)
- Secondary: Black diffusion acrylic for "stealth glow" modules
- Frame: Black anodized MakerBeamXL 15x15mm aluminum extrusion
- Corners: Black PETG (3D printed) → injection molded at volume

**Color Palette**:
- **Primary**: Matte black (`#0A0A0A`)
- **Accent 1**: Teal glow (`#00D9FF`) — inherited from Sacred Circuits brand
- **Accent 2**: Gold circuit traces (`#FFB800`) — subtle, on PCBs
- **Smoke**: Translucent gray (`#808080` at 30% opacity)

**Aesthetic Keywords**:
- Cyberpunk
- Industrial
- Minimal
- Transparent systems
- Visible intelligence
- Stealth technology
- Sacred geometry (subtle grid patterns)

**NOT this**:
- ❌ Consumer electronics (white plastic, glossy)
- ❌ Apple minimalism (too clean, too friendly)
- ❌ RGB gamer aesthetic (too loud)
- ❌ Wooden/warm (wrong emotional tone)
- ❌ Ornamental/baroque (too complex)

### Photography & Rendering Style

**Mood**: Bladerunner meets ancient Greek temple
**Lighting**: Dramatic, high contrast, internal glow as focal point
**Setting**: Dark wall, single light source, smoke/fog optional
**Composition**: Asymmetric grid, colossal scale implied

**Campaign visuals**:
- Show modules in dark room with internal glow only
- Closeups of smoke acrylic transition (off → on)
- Wide shots showing wall as "portal" or "oracle interface"
- Never show in bright white room or with soft shadows

## Brand Voice

**Tone**: Present tense, declarative, technical but poetic
**Style**: Short sentences. Em-dash pivots. Closing questions.
**Inspiration**: McKee storytelling + Sacred Circuits mythology voice

**Example copy**:
> "Each module is a tile. Together, they form an oracle — alive, watching, responding. The wall isn't furniture. It's intelligence that escaped the phone and became physical again. mosAIc doesn't display information. It embodies it."
