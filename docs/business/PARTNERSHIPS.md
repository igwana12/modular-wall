## Partnership Categories

### 1. 3D Printing Partners
Need a partner who can:
- Print high-detail small characters/figurines for shelf display
- Print frames, shelves, corner pieces, enclosures at scale
- Offer on-demand printing for customers who don't own a printer
- Potentially co-brand or white-label

**Types to explore**:
- **Resin printing services** — for detailed miniatures/characters (Formlabs, Elegoo partners)
- **FDM printing farms** — for structural parts (frames, shelves, bricks)
- **On-demand print services** — Shapeways, Craftcloud, PCBWay 3D, JLCPCB 3D, Treatstock
- **Local print farms** — Makerspaces with print-on-demand
- **Printer manufacturers** — Bambu Lab, Prusa, Creality, Elegoo as co-marketing partners (their users are our audience)

**What they'd print**:
- Corner pieces and frame connectors
- Module enclosures (smoke, clear, opaque options)
- Shelf brackets with pogo pin slots
- Filler bricks (plain, art, textured)
- Character figurines for shelves (Pantheon deities, custom designs)
- Decorative gizmos and accessories
- Recycled plastic versions (Bits to Pieces line)
