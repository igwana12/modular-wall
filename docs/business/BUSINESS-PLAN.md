# The Modular Wall — Business Plan

## Executive Summary
A modular, AI-orchestrated media wall system that lets consumers build personalized immersive displays from snap-together modules — hologram fans, screens, pixel art panels, speakers, and ambient lights. Each module magnetically attaches to a wall rail and self-configures. An AI conductor (JARVIS) orchestrates content across all modules based on context, voice commands, and schedules.

**Category**: Consumer hardware + software platform
**Parent Brand**: Sacred Circuits
**Target Launch**: Kickstarter Q4 2026 / Q1 2027
**Seed Budget**: $3-5K (prototypes + video production)

## Manufacturing Strategy

### Prototyping (Self-Funded)
- PCBs: JLCPCB (5-10 unit runs, $2-5 per board)
- Components: AliExpress/LCSC (small quantities)
- Enclosures: 3D printed (own printers or Shapeways)
- Assembly: Manual (self or small team)

### Kickstarter Fulfillment (500-2000 units)
- PCBs: JLCPCB or PCBWay (batch pricing)
- Components: LCSC (volume pricing, 20-50% cheaper)
- Enclosures: injection molding for high-volume modules, 3D print for low-volume
- Assembly: contract manufacturer (Shenzhen) or local assembly partner
- Fulfillment: ShipBob or similar 3PL

### Scale (10,000+ units)
- Full contract manufacturing (PCB + assembly + test + packaging)
- Injection molded enclosures for all module types
- Certified quality testing
- Regional fulfillment centers

## Open Source Strategy
Aligned with Sacred Circuits / Orb open source approach:

**Open (Hardware)**:
- Backplate PCB design (KiCad files)
- Mechanical CAD (STL/STEP files)
- Module connector specification
- Reference firmware (base classes)

**Proprietary (Software)**:
- AI orchestration engine
- TouchDesigner content templates
- Configuration app
- Content marketplace platform
- Premium content packs (deity holograms, sacred geometry)

This creates a flywheel: open hardware drives module ecosystem → more modules drive software subscriptions → software revenue funds R&D.

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Manufacturing delays | HIGH | MEDIUM | Start manufacturing early, buffer 2-3 months |
| WiFi reliability issues | MEDIUM | HIGH | Extensive testing, fallback to wired pogo pin data |
| Price sensitivity ($99/module) | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Lead with $49 Glow module, bundle pricing |
| Nanoleaf launches similar product | LOW | HIGH | AI orchestration moat, module diversity, mythology content |
| TouchDesigner complexity for users | MEDIUM | MEDIUM | Build custom app layer on top, hide complexity |
| ESP32 supply chain issues | LOW | HIGH | Maintain 3-month component buffer, dual-source |
