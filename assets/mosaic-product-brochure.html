The Thesis

As Software Ate the World, AI Is Throwing It Back Up

Software took the physical world and compressed it behind glass. The globe on your desk became Google Maps. The clock on your wall became a phone widget. The photo wall became Instagram. The stereo became Spotify. The calendar, the weather station, the health monitor — all eaten by apps, all trapped inside a 6-inch rectangle.

Now AI dissolves the barrier that kept them there. The programming knowledge that locked hardware to specialists? Gone. A 16-year-old can describe what they want, AI writes the firmware, they print the case, and snap a physical module onto their wall.

The things the phone stole from the physical world — we're giving them back. And now they're alive.

Product Overview

What Is mosAIc?

mosAIc is a modular, snap-together wall computer built from mixed screens, holograms, speakers, sensors, and lights. Each module magnetically attaches to a wall surface and self-configures. An AI conductor orchestrates content across all modules based on context, voice commands, and schedules.

Open hardware. Proprietary soul.

11 Value Propositions

1. Ambient Light System

LED panels that shift with circadian rhythm, weather, mood, time of day. Warm sunrise gradient in the morning, cool blue for focus, warm amber at night.

2. Audio System

Distributed speakers across the wall — spatial audio from multiple positions creates immersive soundscapes.

3. Smart Home Dashboard

Plugs into Home Assistant, Matter, Thread. The wall IS your smart home interface.

4. Health & Wellness Monitor

Beautiful visual interfaces showing sleep quality, heart rate trends, exercise, and custom health metrics from Apple Health, Fitbit, Oura Ring.

5. Education Platform

The wall teaches you how to build and control IoT devices with AI. Each module you add teaches electronics fundamentals, 3D printing, networking, AI integration, and programming.

How It Works

Three Steps to Living Intelligence

1. Choose Your Modules

Start with a starter kit or build a custom configuration. Every wall is unique — yours reflects your life, your workflow, your aesthetic.

2. Snap Together

Magnetic pogo pin connectors. No wiring, no soldering, no programming. Modules snap to a wall-mounted surface and auto-configure. Hot-swappable — rearrange anytime.

3. AI Orchestrates

The Wall Controller Agent discovers each module, routes content intelligently, and creates choreographed scenes. Morning mode. Focus mode. Movie night. Sleep mode. The wall knows what you need before you ask.
