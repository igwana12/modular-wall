## The Name

### mosAIc

- **Casing**: `mosAIc` — lowercase m, capital A and I, lowercase c
- **Pronunciation**: "mo-ZAY-ik" (same as "mosaic")
- **Etymology**: Mosaic (ancient art of assembling small tiles into a larger picture) + AI (artificial intelligence embedded in the name)
- **Why it works**:
  - Each module is a tile. Together they form an intelligent mosaic.
  - Ancient art form + modern AI = the bridge between analog and digital (First Waver thesis)
  - Short, memorable, visual, global (works in every language)
  - The AI is hidden in plain sight — just like the intelligence hidden inside each module

### Usage Rules
| Context | Correct | Incorrect |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Logo / brand mark | mosAIc | Mosaic, MOSAIC, MosAIc, mosaIc |

### Logo Concepts

The mosAIc logo should evoke:
1. **Tiles/grid** — the modular grid pattern
2. **AI highlight** — the "AI" in the name visually distinct
3. **Glow** — teal accent glow suggesting intelligence
4. **Minimal** — works at 16px favicon size

#### Logo Treatment Options

**Option A — Typographic**
```
mos AI c
```
The "AI" in a different color (teal) or weight (bold) while "mos" and "c" are lighter. Clean, readable, scales well.

**Option B — Tile Grid**
A small 2x2 or 3x3 grid of colored squares (like actual mosaic tiles) next to the wordmark. One tile glows teal.

**Option C — Connected Tiles**
The letters themselves are shaped like modular tiles with pogo pin connector dots between them. The "AI" tiles glow.

**Option D — Mosaic Pattern**
The word "mosAIc" with the letters filled with a mosaic tile texture pattern. The "AI" section uses teal tiles, the rest uses dark tiles.

## Photography & Image Style

### Product Photography
- **Background**: Dark (#0D0D1A or matte black studio)
- **Lighting**: Dramatic side light (key), cool fill, teal accent/rim light
- **Lens**: 85mm f/2.8, shallow depth of field
- **Mood**: Premium, minimal, cyberpunk
- **Key details to show**: Gold pogo pins, teal screen glow, material texture

### Lifestyle Photography
- **Setting**: Modern minimalist interiors (dark walls, concrete, warm wood)
- **Lighting**: Mixed — warm interior + cool teal electronic glow
- **Lens**: 35mm wide for room shots, 50mm for detail
- **People**: Hands interacting with modules (building, snapping, touching)
- **Scale reference**: Always show human hand or common object for size context

### Render Style (Blender)
- **Housing material**: Dark matte (Base Color 0.04/0.04/0.05, Roughness 0.85)
- **Screen glow**: Emission 2.5, module's brand color
- **Pogo pins**: Gold metallic (0.83/0.69/0.22, Metallic 1.0)
- **Magnets**: Silver metallic (0.75/0.75/0.78, Metallic 1.0)
- **Studio**: 3-point lighting — key (warm), fill (cool), rim (teal)
- **Camera**: 50-85mm, 3/4 angle, no DOF for product shots

## Voice & Tone

### Writing Style
- **Direct**: "Snap it on. It works." Not "Simply attach the module to the surface."
- **Technical but accessible**: Use real specs but explain why they matter
- **Confident**: "Nobody makes this." Not "We believe we're unique."
- **Short sentences**: Average 12-15 words per sentence
- **Active voice**: "The wall learns your patterns" not "Your patterns are learned by the wall"
- **Em-dash pivots**: Use for contrast and revelation (Niko's signature style)

### Forbidden Words
- "Smart" (overused — every product is "smart")
- "Revolutionary" (hyperbolic)
- "Seamless" (vague)
- "Cutting-edge" (cliche)
- "Ecosystem" (corporate)
- "Leverage" (business jargon)

### Encouraged Words
- Snap, click, glow, pulse, breathe
- Build, craft, assemble, create
- Tile, mosaic, grid, pattern
- Think, sense, learn, anticipate
- Open, modular, magnetic, physical
