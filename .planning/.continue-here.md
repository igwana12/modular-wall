## Design Contract (from user, canonical)

- **Default:** 3 polygons (small/medium/large) + 2 satellites each with trails, proportional size
- **Listening:** All 3 shapes shift warm amber (muted, NOT aggressive red/orange)
- **Speaking:** Oscilloscope replaces smallest shape (TODO: reimplement as Three.js geometry). If symbol referenced, medium morphs to 3D object via particle transition, small fades to 10%
- **Zeus Face:** Replaces large sphere only. Medium 50%, small 10%. No other visuals unless specifically referenced.
- **One object at a time.** Never two GLBs simultaneously.
- **All wireframes centered, facing camera, contained in sphere.**
- **Particle morph transition between every shape change.**
- **Side button:** 1x = PTT, 2x = sleep, 3x = shutdown (NOT WORKING YET)
- **Deformation:** Subtle, smooth — no spikes. Multipliers capped at intensity*1.5.
