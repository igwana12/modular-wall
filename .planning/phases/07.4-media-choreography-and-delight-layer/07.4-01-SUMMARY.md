## Deviations from Plan

None -- plan executed exactly as written.

## Decisions Made

1. **CSS transitions via class toggle (not keyframes)** -- Simpler state management. Three states per transition (initial, .visible, .exiting) controlled by classList rather than @keyframes. This keeps the transition library composable with existing panel show/hide logic.

2. **clip-path for reveal and wipe** -- Uses GPU-accelerated clip-path instead of overflow/translate tricks. No reflow, smooth on all panels regardless of content.

3. **Batch drain with splice snapshot** -- _drain() now snapshots the entire queue with splice(0) before iterating with stagger delays, preventing late-arriving messages from being starved during a cascade.
