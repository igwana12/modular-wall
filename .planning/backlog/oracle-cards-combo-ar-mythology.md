# Oracle Cards: Myth-Specific Stories + AR Reenactments + Combo Mechanics

## Core Concept

Each of the 21 Oracle Cards tells a specific myth where that god is the protagonist. The card design, content, and QR code are unique to that myth — not just the god, but the *story*.

### Single Card Experience
- Scan the QR code → AR 3D animated reenactment of that god's myth plays on screen
- The myth hovers above/around the physical card via AR camera
- Each card is a self-contained story artifact

### Combo Card Mechanic (The Hook)
- Many Greek myths involve multiple gods (Zeus + Hera, Athena + Poseidon, etc.)
- If two gods share a myth, **both physical cards must be present and both QR codes scanned** to unlock the combined story
- The combo AR shows both gods interacting — the full myth reenacted
- Single-card scan shows that god's perspective; both-card scan shows the complete story

### Economic / Collectibility Layer
- **Rarity tiers** for myths (common single-god myths, rare two-god combos, legendary three-god combos?)
- **Incomplete combos create desire** — you have Zeus's card but need Hera's to see their full myth
- **Trading/gifting incentive** — "I have Athena, do you have Poseidon? Let's see the Contest for Athens together"
- **Ranking system** for gods and stories — which myths are most viewed, which combos most unlocked
- **Mystery/sealed pack distribution** — you don't know which myths you'll get, creating unboxing excitement
- **Digital completion tracker** — see which combos you've unlocked, which you're missing

## Why This Works

1. **Collectibility** — TCG mechanics (Magic: The Gathering, Pokemon) proven to drive repeat purchases
2. **Social** — combos require physical proximity with another person's cards, creating shared moments
3. **Discovery** — each card contains a real myth, educational value layered under game mechanics
4. **Scarcity** — if 21 gods × 5 myths each = 105 cards, combo possibilities explode
5. **AR as reveal** — the tech isn't the product, the *story* is. AR is just the delivery mechanism
6. **Network effects** — value of your collection increases as others collect too

## Open Questions (Need Research)

1. **Myth mapping** — which Greek myths pair which gods? How many natural 2-god and 3-god combos exist?
2. **AR tech** — WebAR (8th Wall, AR.js) vs native (ARKit/ARCore)? Can two QR codes trigger a combined experience?
3. **Multi-QR detection** — technically, how does one camera detect two QR codes simultaneously and trigger a combo?
4. **Economy design** — TCG economic models (packs, rarity, trading). What works for a 21-god deck vs 100+ card TCG?
5. **Production scaling** — if each myth = unique card art, that's 105+ unique designs vs 21. Cost/timeline implications.
6. **Ranking mechanics** — ELO for myths? Community voting? Usage-based popularity?
7. **Digital twin** — should there be a digital version of each card you "own" in the app?
