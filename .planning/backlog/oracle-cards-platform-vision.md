# Oracle Cards: The Platform Vision

The cards are not the product. The cards are the *key* to an expanding mythology world.

## Core Mechanic: Combinations Unlock Stories

The more cards you collect, the more myth stories you can watch — but only when cards are physically together. This is the engine:

- **1 card** → that god's origin story (solo AR)
- **2 cards together** → the shared myth between those gods (combo AR)
- **3 cards together** → epic myth (Trojan War scenes, Olympus council, etc.)
- **Full pantheon subset** → legendary content (the Titanomachy, the creation of humanity)

The combinations are the content unlock mechanism. You NEED friends with cards to see the best stories. This drives:
- Repeat purchases (packs to complete combos)
- Social play (bring cards together IRL)
- Trading and gifting (you have what I need)
- Group experiences (gather 5 friends with cards for a legendary unlock)

## Geo-Fenced Content: Location-Based Unlocks

Certain AR stories only unlock at real-world locations tied to Greek mythology or culture:

### Archaeological Sites
- **Delphi** → Apollo's Oracle content (exclusive prophecy reading + AR of the Pythia)
- **Olympia** → Zeus lightning bolt ceremony AR
- **Eleusis** → Persephone/Demeter Mysteries reveal
- **Cape Sounion** → Poseidon temple, exclusive sea myth AR
- **Parthenon/Acropolis** → Athena's birth from Zeus (legendary, only here)

### Museums
- **British Museum** (Elgin Marbles) → Athena + Poseidon contest for Athens
- **National Archaeological Museum, Athens** → multiple deity unlocks near their statues
- **Louvre** (Venus de Milo) → Aphrodite exclusive
- **Met Museum** (Greek wing) → rotating seasonal unlocks

### Other Locations
- **Any Greek restaurant** → Dionysus wine story unlock (fun/casual)
- **Libraries** → Athena wisdom unlock
- **Observatories/planetariums** → Apollo/Artemis celestial myths
- **Near the ocean** → Poseidon content unlocks near any coastline

### How Geo-Fencing Works
- Phone GPS + card scan = location-aware AR
- Content is cached on device after first unlock (works offline after)
- "Passport" in the app tracks which locations you've visited
- Some content is permanently unlocked after visiting; some is location-only

## Social Play Mechanics

### Multiplayer AR Sessions
- One kid holds the phone/iPad, others hold cards in frame
- The AR story plays across ALL cards visible to the camera
- Kids literally gather around, holding their cards up, watching gods interact
- The social moment IS the product

### Classroom / Educational Mode
- Teacher has a "Mythology Pack" with all 21 base cards
- Students each get assigned a god
- Class activity: combine cards to discover myths
- Curriculum-aligned content for each myth (age-appropriate)
- Schools are a B2B channel

### Family Play
- Parents and kids discover myths together
- Bedtime ritual: scan tonight's card, watch the story, discuss
- Ties directly to the bedtime story pipeline (already built)

## Economic Structure

### Tier 1: Base Oracle Deck ($29.99)
- 21 cards, one per god
- Solo origin stories for each god
- Basic daily oracle reading via QR
- ~10 two-card combos included

### Tier 2: Myth Packs ($9.99 each)
- 5 cards per pack, themed by myth cycle
- "Trojan War Pack" — Athena, Ares, Aphrodite, Hera, Zeus war-specific cards
- "Underworld Pack" — Hades, Persephone, Hecate, Hermes (psychopomp), Demeter
- "Love & Desire Pack" — Aphrodite, Eros, Dionysus, Apollo, Pan
- Each pack unlocks 3-5 new combo stories
- Randomized rare insert (1 in 4 packs gets a "golden" variant card)

### Tier 3: Legendary Cards ($4.99 each, limited)
- Holographic / foil finish
- Unlock the rarest AR stories
- Seasonal drops (solstice, equinox, Greek holidays)
- Numbered editions

### Tier 4: Location Passport ($14.99/year subscription)
- Unlocks geo-fenced content
- Tracks visited locations
- Exclusive AR for archaeological sites + museums
- Revenue share with museums/sites possible

### Tier 5: Classroom License ($99/year)
- All cards digitally
- Teacher dashboard
- Curriculum guides
- 30-student classroom mode

## Rarity & Ranking System

### God Power Rankings
- Community-voted + algorithm-weighted
- Based on: readings given, combo stories unlocked, cards in circulation
- Monthly leaderboard: "Most Consulted Oracle This Month"
- Gods gain "power" as more people collect and use their cards

### Story Rarity
- **Common** — single-god stories (every card has one)
- **Uncommon** — 2-god combos (46 possible, most accessible)
- **Rare** — 3-god combos (19 mapped, need specific trio)
- **Epic** — location-locked stories (require physical travel)
- **Legendary** — full pantheon events (5+ cards together, seasonal)

### Card Rarity
- **Standard** — base art
- **Holographic** — same art, foil treatment
- **Golden** — alternate art, gold foil border, numbered
- **Mythic** — animated card face (digital only? or lenticular print?)

## Why This Works as a Platform

1. **Content is the moat** — 21 gods × hundreds of myths = years of expansion content
2. **Physical + digital hybrid** — cards are physical (tangible, tradeable), stories are digital (scalable, updatable)
3. **Location partnerships** — museums and sites WANT foot traffic; this drives it
4. **Education market** — Greek mythology is in every school curriculum
5. **Social by design** — combo mechanic requires IRL togetherness
6. **Recurring revenue** — packs, subscriptions, seasonal drops
7. **Cultural relevance** — mythology is timeless; not a fad
8. **AI personalization** — the oracle reading (Phase 2) personalizes every interaction

## Relationship to Current Products

- **Oracle Cards v1** (Phase 3) = base deck, oracle readings, no AR yet. Market validation.
- **Oracle Engine** (Phase 4-5) = voice AI device. Independent product track.
- **This platform vision** = the expansion plan for Oracle Cards after v1 validates.
- **POV Globe / Spirit Sphere** = premium hardware for the most dedicated fans.

The platform vision doesn't delay current work. It gives Phase 3's v1 deck a massive growth trajectory.
